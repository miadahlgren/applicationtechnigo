Responsive Design

Want your design to look good on all different kinds of devices? Tired of having to scroll for 450 years on your phone to get to the end of a webpage? Maybe then Responsive Design may interest you.

Respronsive design is an approach within web design that focuses on making sites, applications and systems look good on different kinds of electronic devices. The aim is to make one source of information automatically adapt to the device it is currently viewed on. This is done by adding different attributes to the code.
